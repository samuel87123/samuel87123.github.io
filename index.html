a shooting game makes players know more about body by a easy way

Players control the marrow ship -- the cell producing factory -- to protect body from germs around us

There are two modes of the game, one is bacteria, and the other is virus

In first mode, bacteria invade body and reproduce if players don’t destroy them in time

In second mode, in addition to reproducing , virus will also infect cells

Infected cells

Players can shoot cells to fight

White blood cells can swallow bacteria and virus

T cells can kill the cells infected by virus

Plasma cells can produce antibodies

Antibodies can slow down the speed of virus
